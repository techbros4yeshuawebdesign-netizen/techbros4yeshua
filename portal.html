    Frequently Asked Questions
    What counts as an edit request?+
    An edit is any change to your existing site — text updates, image swaps, color changes, moving sections around, or adding a small new element. New pages or major redesigns are separate requests.
    How many edits do I get per month?+
    Starter: 2 edits/month. Professional: 4 edits/month. Premium: Unlimited edits. Extra edits on Starter/Pro are $10 each.
    How long does an edit take?+
    Most edits are completed within 3–5 business days. Complex changes may take longer. You'll see your request status update in the Edit Requests tab.
    What does maintenance include?+
    Maintenance covers: security updates, hosting uptime monitoring, SSL certificate renewal, and regular backups. It does NOT include new features, integrations, or redesigns.
    How do I approve my prototype?+
    You have 7 days from when your prototype is ready to review and request changes. On day 7 we proceed with the current version and begin your subscription. Use the Edit Requests tab for any remaining changes.
    Can I upgrade my plan?+
    Yes! Contact Nathan or email techBros4yeshua.web.design@gmail.com to upgrade. Your old plan cancels and the new one starts — no double billing.
    What if my site goes down?+
    Email Nathan immediately at techBros4yeshua.web.design@gmail.com. Hosting issues are treated as urgent and addressed as soon as possible, usually within a few hours.
    Can I cancel my subscription?+
    Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your site stays live until the end of your billing period. After cancellation, hosting ends and the site goes offline.
